## 1. Competitor teardown → your wedge

| Competitor | Model | Price | Weakness you attack |
|---|---|---|---|
| **HomeExchange** | Points ("GuestPoints") + reciprocal | **~$235/yr** | Subscription resentment; GuestPoints "too many points, hard to spend, poor value"; trust fears |
| **People Like Us** | Reciprocal/curated | ~$159/yr | Paywall; smaller network |
| **Love Home Swap** | Subscription swap | Subscription | Paywall |
| **Swaphouse** | Free, WFH/nomad, dating-app match | **Free** | **Closest to swapl** — watch closely; ~1,300 listings, thin outside hubs |
| **PropertySwap.net** | Free, permanent swaps | Free | Different use-case (permanent) |

**The three things HomeExchange users complain about most — and swapl's answer:**
1. **"I have to pay a yearly fee I didn't sign up for."** → swapl: **no fees, ever. Keys for keys.**
2. **"GuestPoints are confusing and hard to spend."** → swapl: **pure reciprocal, no virtual currency to manage.**
3. **"Can I trust a stranger in my home?"** → swapl: **every accepted swap auto-insured + verified hosts.**

### ⚠️ Strategic risk to internalize
HomeExchange *invented GuestPoints because pure reciprocal matching is hard* —
aligning two people who each want the other's city on the same dates rarely
happens organically. swapl's "no money / pure swap" is cleaner messaging but
inherits that exact matching problem. **Your corridor curation + match score is
the mitigation, and it's not optional — it's the whole game.** If a new user
searches and sees no plausible match, you lose them to the same wall HomeExchange
papered over with points. This is why pre-launch supply must be *dense in few
corridors*, not spread thin. (Decide deliberately whether swapl ever needs a
points/credits escape hatch for non-overlapping dates — the answer affects supply
liquidity a lot.)

## 2. Facebook groups — how to actually use them

### Don't: post promo or harvest members from competitor-owned groups
"All About Home Exchange (Official)" (HomeExchange's own, 50.1K members) and
similar **explicitly ban** (a) posts about other platforms and (b) soliciting
members — and treat content as confidential. Posting there = auto-removed;
scraping members = ToS + privacy violation + account ban. Not worth it.

### Do: three legitimate plays

**(a) Competitive intel (read-only).** Use competitor groups only to listen —
aggregate pain points, most-requested cities/corridors, recurring objections —
and feed them into copy/FAQ/video. Never extract identities or private posts.

**(b) Recruit in groups that ALLOW it.** Target *independent* / general
communities whose rules permit a value-first founding-host post. Candidate types
to join & verify rules first, then post script B2 from the outreach kit:
- General home-swap / direct-swapper groups (non-platform-owned)
- City expat groups for launch cities ("Expats in Lisbon/Amsterdam/CDMX/Tokyo/…")
- Digital-nomad groups ("Digital Nomads Around The World", city nomad groups)
- Sabbatical / academic housing groups (SabbaticalHomes community)
- Remote-work & coliving communities
> Always read pinned rules first. If promo is restricted, lead with pure value
> (the cost-comparison post, B1-style) and let people ask.

**(c) Build a swapl-owned group — own the audience.** See §3.

## 3. Swapl-owned Facebook group — launch plan

Owning the community means no one can ban you from your own funnel, and it
compounds. Mirror what makes the competitor group work (50K, 24 posts/day) but
build it around *your* corridors.

**Name:** "Swapl — Home Swappers (No Fees, Insured)" or "Keys for Keys — Home
Swap Community."

**Seeding (week 1):** invite your waitlist + every founding host; post the first
5–10 "swap reveal" pieces yourself so the feed isn't empty on day one.

**Starter rules (keep it trustworthy):**
1. Home-swap discussion & founding-host listings welcome.
2. Real names only; be respectful.
3. Share your city + what corridor you want (helps matching).
4. No rentals / no off-topic selling.
5. Report problems to admins; what's shared in good faith stays in the group.

**Weekly cadence:** Mon corridor spotlight · Wed "who wants to swap with X?" match
thread · Fri swap-reveal video · monthly "new city unlocked" announcement.

**Cross-pollinate:** every value-first post in *other* allowed groups ends with
"join the swapl community group for [city] swaps" → pulls borrowed audiences into
owned audience.
