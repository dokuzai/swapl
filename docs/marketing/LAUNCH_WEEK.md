## T‑0 — Launch day
Sequence the day; don't fire everything at 9am.

**1) Email blast (morning) — subject A/B:** "It's live: trade keys for keys 🔑" / "Home swapping is open in [City]"
> It's live. Swaps are open in **[cities]** — keys for keys, no money, every accepted
> stay insured end to end.
> • Browse your matches, ranked by fit
> • Propose a swap in two taps
> • Founding hosts show first
> **[Open swapl → swapl.fun/listings?utm_source=email&utm_medium=lifecycle&utm_campaign=launch_day]**
> Thank you for being early. This only works because you listed first.

**2) Product Hunt (00:01 PT) — tagline:** "Swapl — Home swapping. No money, every stay insured."
> First comment (founder): "Hi PH 👋 We built Swapl because travel got expensive and
> Airbnb fatigue is real. It's reciprocal home swapping — you trade your home for
> someone else's, no nightly rates, no subscription. The two things that always
> killed home swapping — trust and finding a match — we fixed by (1) auto-insuring
> every accepted swap + verifying hosts, and (2) opening city-by-city so real matches
> exist on day one. Live now in [cities]. AMA."
> Rally your waitlist + community to upvote/comment in the first 3 hours.

**3) Social blast (mid-morning):** the swap-reveal hero video (Storyboard 1) pinned. Same vertical asset to TikTok/Reels/Shorts + a still carousel on Instagram/LinkedIn.
**4) Founder post (LinkedIn/X):** the "why we built this" story (mirror the PH comment), link to the homepage.
**5) Reddit:** a genuine launch post in r/digitalnomad / city subs where you've built goodwill — lead with the cost + insurance story, not "we launched."
**6) Community:** "WE'RE LIVE" pin; spotlight the first real swap as it happens.
**7) Paid:** flip on the **demand** campaigns for ready corridors (retarget waitlist first — see PAID_ADS_SETUP.md §3).

## Guardrails
- Segment email: **listed** hosts get "browse your matches"; **not-listed** get "list now, founding status." Never send the wrong CTA.
- Don't announce a corridor that fails the ≥5-match gate — an empty search on launch day is the one unrecoverable mistake.
- Keep every link UTM'd; judge the week by `BetaSignup` + proposal data, not platform vanity metrics.
