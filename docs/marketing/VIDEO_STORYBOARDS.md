# Swapl — Video Storyboards (shot-by-shot, ready to shoot)

_Turns the 5 scripted formats in [CHANNELS_AND_OUTREACH.md](CHANNELS_AND_OUTREACH.md)
into shot lists a creator (or you) can shoot today._ Vertical 9:16, one asset →
TikTok + Reels + Shorts.

## Global rules
- **Hook in 0–2s.** Lead with a price number or a question, never a logo.
- **Length:** 15–35s. TikTok = raw/authentic; Reels = "elevated casual"; Shorts = punchy.
- **Captions always on** (most viewers are muted). Big, high-contrast, navy/pink.
- **One CTA**, spoken + on-screen + in caption: "Link in bio — list before September."
- **Specs:** 1080×1920, 30fps, safe margins (keep text out of top 220px / bottom 320px where the UI sits), MP4 H.264.
- **Bio link:** point at a corridor page with UTM, e.g.
  `swapl.fun/swap/lisbon-home-swap-from-cdmx?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=video&utm_campaign=founding_hosts`

## Production & posting
- **Batch-shoot:** one afternoon in one swap home yields formats 1, 2, 4 + b-roll for 3 & 5.
- **Cadence:** 1–3/day across all 3 platforms. Rotate formats; never post the same one twice in a week.
- **Creator seeding > paid reach:** send founding-host creators (10k–100k, in your corridor cities) the offer; they shoot format 1 in their own home → you get supply AND reach.
- **Hooks to A/B (first 2s):** price number · "POV you paid $0" · "whose place?" · "a stranger in my home?". Keep the winners; the hook is 80% of performance.
- **Repurpose:** the top performer becomes a paid ad (UGC ads outperform polished). Storyboard 3 first.
